FAQ

Fair questions.

What exactly is a GO?
A GO is a customizable avatar NFT minted on the XRP Ledger. Each one is composited from interchangeable traits across eight slots on a growing roster of body types. Only 10,000 GOs will ever exist — customization changes what a GO looks like, never how many there are.
Do I need a browser wallet or a trustline?
No. That's the whole point. You use the web app or a bot in Discord or Telegram, and every transaction is signed in your own Xaman wallet on your phone. Our servers never see your private keys — they only ever receive a transaction you already signed.
What does minting cost?
Pricing can move with the market, so you always see the current cost before you commit — check the web app, or ask the bot in Discord or Telegram.
Can I change my GO after minting?
Yes. Pick two GOs you own with the same body type and swap traits between them. Swaps happen on-chain via NFTokenModify (XLS-46), which updates the NFT in place — your GO keeps its identity and edition number while the artwork changes.
Where can I verify all this on-chain?
Everything we mint is tagged with SourceTag 2606160021, so you can trace our activity on any XRPL explorer. The collection lives at xrp.cafe/collection/lfg.
